Problem Statement

Lending Club co NPL Ratio dat dinh 19.07% (2015) va tong ECL theo PD tinh chi la $1,122M — con so nay bi underestimate nghiem trong do PD nhom Late (debt_group 3) la 35% trong khi thuc nghiem la 100% (sai lech 65 diem %). Bai toan: lam the nao de xay dung he thong phan tich rui ro chinh xac hon, co kha nang canh bao som truoc khi no xau phat sinh, va tinh toan ECL dung chuan IFRS 9 voi ML-PD thay vi PD gia dinh cung?

1 Chan doan Danh muc — Hieu boi canh truoc khi mo hinh hoa

Truoc khi xay dung bat ky mo hinh nao, can hieu ro suc khoe hien tai cua danh muc, cac yeu to vi mo anh huong den no xau, va dac tinh lich su theo thoi gian. 4 module sau (M1, M3, M4, M5) phuc vu muc tieu nay.

M1 — Portfolio Health: NPL & Grade Mix

Phat hien chinh: NPL tang don dieu tu Grade A (3.59%) den Grade G (39.41%) — he thong phan hang co gia tri phan biet rui ro. Loi nghiep vu da phat hien: flag logic goc gan nhan OK cho Grade D-G du NPL 20-39% vi chi kiem tra portfolio share, khong kiem tra NPL. Da sua thanh dual-dimension flag.

NPL_Ratio = (Charged Off / Total Loans) × 100 concentration_flag = WATCH si portfolio_share > 10% | ALERT si > 20% credit_quality_flag = WATCH si NPL > 8% | HIGH_RISK si > 20% composite_flag = HIGH_RISK neu bat ky chieu nao canh bao
  • Grade D (NPL 20.29%), E (28.17%), F (36.20%), G (39.41%) — da duoc flag HIGH_RISK chinh xac sau fix.
  • NPL Trend: tang tu 2007, dat dinh 19.07% (2015-03), giam dan — dung chu ky tin dung Lending Club.
  • debt_consolidation: ALERT_BOTH (concentration + credit risk). credit_card: CONCENTRATION only (NPL binh thuong).

M3 — Migration Matrix: Lich su Dich chuyen Nhom No

Phat hien chinh: Xac suat Nhom 3 (Late) chuyen sang Nhom 5 (Charged Off) la 15.05% — cao gap 4.6 lan so voi Nhom 1→Nhom 5 (3.28%). Day la tin hieu quan trong cho EWS. Loi da sua: diagonal 27.07% la point-in-time snapshot, khong phai Markov transition probability.

Annualized_NPL = NPL_raw × (12 / avg_loan_age_months) Reliable cohort: avg_loan_age ≥ 36 thang → 2010–2015 NPL annualized TB (reliable): 4.57%/nam — ben vung, co the dung calibrate
  • Cohort 2010-2015 NPL annualized = 4.57%/nam — benchmark de thiết lap provision rate.
  • Cohort 2017-2018 NPL thap (3-11%) la truncation bias, khong phai chat luong tot hon.

M4 — Macro & Concentration: DTI va Lag Effect

Insight quan trong nhat toan du an: Corr NPL vs Unemployment tai lag=0 chi la 0.271 (yeu), nhung tai lag=24 thang dat 0.651 — co nghia la chinh sach tin dung can theo doi Unemployment Rate hom nay de du bao NPL sau 2 nam.

r(lag=k) = Corr(NPL_t, Unemployment_{t-k}) Lag toi uu = argmax |r| → lag=24 thang (r=0.651) DTI Bucket (loan-level, 0-40%): Pearson r = +0.953 → DTI tang 5% → NPL tang ~1.9 diem % (Loai DTI > 100%: 4,140 khoan du lieu loi) DTI 45-60%: selection bias — underwriting nghiem ngat, NPL thap la do loc
r = 0.953
Tuong quan NPL vs DTI trong vung 0-40% — bien du bao manh nhat trong toan du an

M5 — Credit Appetite: Score Gap theo Tieu bang

FICO Gap giam tu >100 diem (2007-2012) xuong 35-80 diem (2013-2018) — credit appetite noi long ro rang sau khung hoang. Phat hien loi: cold-start thang 2007-05 (rejection 100% do 0 accepted, khong phai policy) va tail artifact cuoi 2018 (dataset bi cat) da duoc loc.

  • Score Gap vs Rejection Rate: r=0.474 — state gap cao → rejection cao, dung huong.
  • DC gap cao nhat (89.8 pts): underwriting nghiem ngat nhat hoac population rui ro cao nhat.
  • Score gap la so tuyet doi giua 2 thang do khac nhau — chi so sanh tuong doi giua cac state.
2 Xay dung Mo hinh — PD Model va Early Warning System

Voi nen tang hieu biet ve danh muc tu Tier 1, Tier 2 xay dung hai mo hinh co gia tri thuc thi: mo hinh PD du bao xac suat vo no ca nhan hoa, va EWS phat hien som rui ro truoc khi no xau phat sinh.

M6 — PD Model: Gradient Boosting AUC=0.9702

Ket qua dinh luong: Gradient Boosting dat Test AUC=0.9702, Gini=0.9403, KS=0.871 — vuot toan bo nguong production (Basel II >0.75, thuc tien tot >0.85). Mo hinh duoc train tren time-based split nghiem ngat (Train 2007-2015, Val 2016, Test 2017-2018) de tranh data leakage theo chu ky kinh te.

M7 — EWS: Tu Circular AUC 0.999 den Predictive AUC 0.7412

Phat hien quan trong nhat cua du an: AUC 0.999 cua EWS goc la gia tri gia do circular evaluation — Signal 1 dung debt_group lam input, trong khi label cung tu debt_group. Da xu ly bang cach xay dung True Predictive EWS voi 4 features doc lap, cho AUC=0.7412 co y nghia thuc su.

EWS Predictive Score (khong dung debt_group): ews = fico_deterioration×0.35 + dti_signal×0.25 + delinq_signal×0.25 + revol_signal×0.15 fico_deterioration = |last_fico - fico_range_low| / 300 dti_signal = min(dti / 50, 1.0) [cap 50% = nguong nguy hiem] delinq_signal = delinq_2yrs×0.3 + ever_delinquent×0.7 revol_signal = revol_util / 100.0

3 Ung dung IFRS 9 — Gia tri Dinh luong cua ML trong Du phong

Day la tang ket qua — noi Tier 1 (hieu biet danh muc) va Tier 2 (mo hinh PD va EWS) hoi tu de tra loi cau hoi kinh doanh chinh: ML-PD thay doi muc du phong IFRS 9 len bao nhieu so voi phuong phap truyen thong?

M2 — Baseline IFRS 9: PD Tinh 5-bucket

PD tinh theo debt_group: Group 3 (Late) = 35% trong khi thuc nghiem la 100% (underestimate 65 diem %). Ket qua: baseline ECL $1,122M la con so sai do flat PD assumption.

Empirical PD validation: Group 3 (Late 31-120d): static PD = 35% vs empirical PD = 100% → error = -65 pts Group 5 (Charged Off) : static PD = 90% vs empirical PD = 100% → error = -10 pts

M8 — Advanced IFRS 9: ML-PD Integration

M8 tich hop ML-PD tu M6 (calibrated Isotonic), LGD thuc nghiem theo grade (thap hon Basel 12-25 diem %), va EAD net sau recoveries. Ket qua: ECL tang tu $1,122M len $1,526M.

ECL = pd_for_ecl × LGD_adjusted × EAD × Horizon × Discount Stage 1: pd_for_ecl = pd_score | horizon = 1/12 (12-month ECL) Stage 2: pd_for_ecl = pd_score | horizon = remaining_life Stage 3: pd_for_ecl = 1.0 | horizon = 1.0 [IFRS 9 para 5.5.3: confirmed default] EAD Stage 3 = loan_amnt × CCF(0.75) − recoveries [net sau thu hoi] SICR Stage 2: debt_group==2 OR FICO det.>20 OR (pd_score>0.15 AND has_ml_pd=True) [Chi ap PD>15% cho ML-PD thuc, tranh Stage 2 inflation tu PD fill]

3 De xuat Co gia tri Trien khai Ngay

  1. 1
    Tich hop ML-PD Calibrated vao SICR va ECL Production

    Isotonic-calibrated PD (ECE=0.062) da san sang thay the PD tinh. Ap dung SICR filter has_ml_pd=True tranh Stage 2 inflation. Mo rong training sang Current loans (proxy label: payment behavior score) de tang PD coverage tu 59.6% len 95%+.

  2. 2
    Trien khai EWS theo 3 che do van hanh dong

    ALERT (R=75%): quet dinh ky toan danh muc. BALANCED (P=36%, R=37%): van hanh hang tuan. PRECISE (P=42%): kiem tra sau uu tien. Tang trong so Signal 3 (PD proxy) tu 0.2 len 0.35 bang calibrated ML-PD — du kien predictive AUC tang tu 0.741 len 0.82+.

  3. 3
    Xay dung Macro Stress Testing voi Unemployment Lag 24 thang

    Su dung Unemployment Rate (t-24) lam leading indicator du bao NPL tuong lai (r=0.651). Thiet lap DTI cap ≤35% trong underwriting policy (DTI tang 5% → NPL tang 1.9 diem %). Tich hop vao provision rate de ECL phan ung voi chu ky kinh te.
